RETROACTIVE PAYMENTS!
UP TO 5 YEARS

Depending on the territory, we are able to recover revenues up to 5 years old. We issue statements quarterly. When a payment is available, you get paid with just one click and you can access to your statements history with all details from the source.

DIRECT REGISTRATION MAXIMIZING INCOMES AND GETTING FASTER PAYMENTS

We register and collect directly your data with every collection societies. This makes the declaration process much faster and we can easily resolve any issues.

WORLDWIDE COLLECTION

Our network covers 95% of where neighbouring rights revenues are generated worldwide. Going much further than basic registration, we constantly follow-up & monitor your tracks. More about our worldwide network (redirection to FAQ).

EASY ONLINE TOOL

REGISTRATION TRACKING AND INCOME REPORTING ONLINE. ANALYSIS OF KEY DATA: Check where you’re registered, analyse sales statistics per track, country and revenue source. View and download your statements, Correct your repertoire metadata. You can basically monitor everything!

COUNTER CLAIM RESOLUTION

Rights’Up finds out if your revenues are currently being collected by someone else. Manage the conflict with one click, we’ll do the rest and retrieve what is yours! If royalties has been distributed outside of the agencies retroactive years, don’t worry, we’ll chase your money back.

PERSONAL ACCOUNTING

Send us your metadata and we'll take care of the rest! A member of our team will be dedicated to processing your membership with Rights’Up. From uploading your repertoire, implementing your claim rules and declaring sales figures... the process is smooth and efficient.

NO SUBSCRIPTION FEES

No set-up costs. You get 80% of your generated revenues.
